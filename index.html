Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · BakBak by Devarshi Shah

The short version: BakBak is built to be anonymous. We never collect your real name or photo. We use your location only to show you nearby posts. We don't sell your data to anyone, ever.

1. Who We Are

BakBak ("we", "our", "the app") is an anonymous hyperlocal social app developed by Devarshi Shah. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use BakBak on iOS or Android.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to run the app:

  • Google Account ID — used solely to authenticate you. We never see or store your real name, email, or profile photo from Google.
  • Apple Account ID — same as above for Apple Sign In users.
  • Location data — your approximate GPS coordinates are used in real time to show you posts within 1km. We store only a geohash (a coded version of your location) alongside your posts — never your exact coordinates permanently.
  • Posts and votes — the content you post and votes you cast are stored in our database linked to your anonymous user ID, not your real identity.
  • Aura score — your in-app reputation score, stored anonymously.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your real name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile photo
  • Your phone number
  • Your precise permanent location history
  • Any payment or financial information

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To show you anonymous posts from people within 1km of your current location
  • To allow you to post anonymously
  • To calculate and display your Aura score
  • To enforce daily post limits and prevent abuse
  • To hide or remove content that is reported by multiple users

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in the asia-south1 (Mumbai) region. All posts automatically expire and are deleted after 24 hours. User accounts and Aura scores are retained until you delete your account.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database
  • Google Sign In / Apple Sign In — authentication only

7. Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app (My Aura → Delete Account). This permanently removes your profile, Aura score, and all posts.

8. Children's Privacy

BakBak is not intended for users under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used our app, please contact us and we will delete the account.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of BakBak after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: simutomy14@gmail.com

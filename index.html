Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Mach Mach by Devarshi Shah

The short version: Mach Mach is built to be anonymous. We never collect your real name or photo. We use your location only to show you nearby posts. We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.

1. Who We Are

Mach Mach ("we", "our", "the app") is an anonymous hyperlocal social app developed by Devarshi Shah, based in Mumbai, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use Mach Mach on iOS or Android.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to run the app:

  • Apple Account ID — used solely to authenticate you. We never see or store your real name, email address, or profile photo from Apple.
  • Location data — your approximate GPS coordinates are used in real time to show you posts within 1km of your current position. We store only a geohash (a privacy-safe encoded version of your location) alongside your posts — never your exact coordinates permanently.
  • Posts and votes — the content you post and votes you cast are stored in our database linked to your anonymous user ID, not your real identity.
  • Aura score — your in-app reputation score, stored anonymously and never linked to your real identity.
  • Daily post count — a counter to enforce the 5 posts per day limit. This resets every 24 hours.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your real name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile photo
  • Your phone number
  • Your permanent or historical location
  • Any payment or financial information
  • Device contacts, camera, or microphone data
  • Any biometric data

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To show you anonymous posts from people within 1km of your current location
  • To allow you to post content anonymously
  • To calculate and display your Aura reputation score
  • To enforce daily post limits (5 posts per day) and prevent abuse
  • To automatically hide content that has been reported 5 or more times by other users
  • To maintain app security and prevent fraudulent activity

5. Content Moderation

Every post on Mach Mach has a 🚩 report button visible to all users. Posts that receive 5 or more reports are automatically hidden from all feeds immediately. We review severely reported content and reserve the right to permanently delete posts and ban accounts that violate our Terms of Use.

We do not use automated content scanning at this time. Moderation is community-driven through the reporting system.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in the asia-south1 (Mumbai) region. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data. All posts automatically expire and are permanently deleted after 24 hours. User accounts and Aura scores are retained until you choose to delete your account.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We use the following third-party services solely to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase — secure database and user authentication
  • Apple Sign In — authentication only, no profile data is accessed

8. Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete your account — available anytime from within the app (My Aura tab → Delete Account). This permanently and irreversibly removes your profile, Aura score, post history, and all associated data.
  • Revoke location access — you can turn off location permissions in your phone settings at any time. Note that the app requires location to function.
  • Data requests — you may contact us to request a copy of your data or to ask questions about what we store.

9. Children's Privacy

Mach Mach is strictly not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us immediately at the email below and we will delete the account and all associated data promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the app evolves. We will notify users of significant changes through an in-app notice. Your continued use of Mach Mach after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
